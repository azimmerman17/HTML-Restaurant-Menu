Origin Story

As part of the downtown Phoenixville's rebirth, H-Thai_ML was one of the first resuaurants to open during a dramatic change to the Bridge Street landscape. Built with a family friendly envirnment, we saw immediate success giving family's an affordable option for a fun night out. Since, we have expanded to 4 locations in the region and still hold true to creating an affordable family environment.
